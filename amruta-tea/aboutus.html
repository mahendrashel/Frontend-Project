WELCOME TO TEA HOUSE !

Amruta Tea is one amongst the swiftly growing brands in Packaged Tea, a brand owned by Malpani Tea Corporation ,which caters to the discerning rural population of India.

Amruta Tea has earned the reputation of superlative taste and quality in 25 years of its business. Our tea is sourced from reputed tea gardens of India and blended by our veterans to deliver consistent results.

We manufacture and package our tea at a Hi Tech ISO Certified plant that gives our tea a leading edge. Amruta Tea has always propagated Good Life Good Living.
